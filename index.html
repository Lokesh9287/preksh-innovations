About SLV Associates

SLV Associates was started in the year 1996. we feel extremely proud in announcing that we are one of the fastest growing realtors in Bangalore with our primary. being on Residential Apartments. We have been credited over 15 projects in and around Bangalore. We are happy to state that all our projects were given possession to our customers within the promised time frames.

SLV Associates offers a life style of luxury and elegance with creativity. We are focused on offering High quality apartments at affordable costs. our structure safety is considered as our prime concern, we therefore always remained attentive on the civil engineers team who work with us. with the experienced and committed team of engineers and architects we have, we confidently assure the finest Homes to our customers.

OUR QUALITY

We believe in developing and constructing aesthetically designed, functionally efficient residential projects offering more value for money. we believe that our constructions should reflect engineering excellence with a view to providing complete customer satisfaction.Provide highest quality constructions at economical cost, driving towards their utmost satisfaction.
